ABOUT US

Association for Computing Machinery

Our Moto : Advancing Computing as a Science & Profession We see a world where computing helps solve tomorrow’s problems – where we use our knowledge and skills to advance the profession and make a positive impact.

It is an world's largest scientific and educational computing international learned society for computing founded in 1947 headquartered in New York City. The ACM and the IEEE Computer Society are the umbrella organizations for US academic and scholarly interests in computing. Unlike the IEEE, the ACM is solely dedicated to computing.

ACM also sponsors other computer science related events such as the worldwide ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC), and has sponsored some other events such as the chess match between Garry Kasparov and the IBM Deep Blue computer.

Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology

Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology (MAIT) is a private Engineering College, located in New Delhi, India. It is affiliated to the Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University,Delhi which is a public, professional university of Delhi. We have 10 departments (Five-Engineering, Four-Research and Three-Auxiliary) including 900 engineering students of the following branches : CSE, IT, ECE, MAE, EEE. It is amongst the top 5 engineering colleges in New Delhi.
